Yedi Technologies Inc. - Written Assignment

Thank you for applying for the Full Stack Software Engineer position at Yedi Technologies Inc.

Engineering Values

  • Speed with Discipline: We are a startup, so we have to move fast, but that doesn't mean we can afford to be sloppy with our practices.
  • Quality over Quantity: We like to be intentional and focused about why and how we build things. As such, we need to make sure what we ship is reliable and scalable.
  • Transparency: We seek and tell the truth, even where those truths are painful or inconvenient.
  • Ownership and Autonomy: As a mostly remote company, we can't constantly monitor how each of us works. We want people who are self-driven and proactive about their work.
  • Problem-Solving Mindset: We want people who like to challenge themselves to solve new problems. Sometimes this also means sharing ideas and collaborating on solutions!
  • Balanced Scalability: We are building with thousands, soon millions, of daily users in mind. We need to build robust systems that accommodate our operational and financial circumstances as a startup.

Instructions:

  1. Submit a written response (in PDF format) addressing the following question:

    2. These values are not always in harmony with each other. Tell us about a time in your career or education where you had to balance between two of these values and how you handled it. (Max word count: 500)

  2. If you use generative AI tools, please include both the prompt and the answers generated.
  3. Send your response to applications@yedi.app.

Deadline: Please submit your response by January 12 2025.

If your submission meets our expectations, we will invite you to proceed to the next round of our interview process.
